About me

I am currently a post-doctoral researcher at the Software Languages Lab of the Vrije Universiteit Brussel with Prof. Coen De Roover. In 2015, I received my M.Sc. degree from the ENSIAS school of the University of Mohammed V in Rabat. In 2019, obtained my Ph.D. in computer science from the University of Mons under the supervision of Prof. Tom Mens. My research focuses mainly on empirical software engineering, in particular software health and evolution, mining software repositories and software analytics. I am also interested in information security and data analysis.

My research interests include:
  • Mining software repositories
  • Software ecosystems
  • Software evolution
  • software analytics
  • Software health
